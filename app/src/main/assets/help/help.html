• Bookmarked cities

    You can bookmark location to see the weather updates.

    • Tap on the map icon on the home screen
    • It will open the google map screen
    • Tap on your desired position on map
    • Click on the marker to add as Favourite
    • Alternatively, keep on writing your desired place in the box
    • Select a city from the suggestion to add as bookmark

  • Remove Bookmark

    Tap and hold on any city; a dialog will open asking for delete.

  • Weather Forecast

    You can see the forecast for next 4 days once you click on a bookmarked cities from the list.

  • Settings

    Open the settings menu from the menu bar.

    You can clear the bookmarked cities or change the unit system (Metric or Imperial).
